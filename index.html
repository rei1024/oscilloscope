Note

  • Supported rules
    • Outer-totalistic rules (range 1 Moore and von Neumann)
    • Isotropic non-totalistic (INT) rules (range 1 Moore)
    • Non isotropic (MAP) rules (range 1 Moore)
  • Rules containing B0 (birth with 0 neighbors) are not supported.
  • The supplied pattern must be a phase of an oscillator or a spaceship.
  • The maximum period that can be analyzed is 50,000.
  • The maximum world size is 8192x8192.
